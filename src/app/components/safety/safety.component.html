Tips and tricks

Research

Review the profiles of the guests and hosts you’ll be working with. We keep a safe and trustworthy marketplace, but only connect with people you feel confident meeting in person.

Review

Rate and review hosts & guests after each trip to help other members make informed decisions when they travel or share their cars.

Protect yourself

Never post your address, email, or phone number online. We keep your private information private, and only reveal members’ names, numbers, and locations once a trip is booked.

Communication is Key

Stay in touch with your host or guest. Clear communication helps build trust and ensures a smooth experience during your trip.

Preparedness

Know the emergency procedures and contact information. Familiarize yourself with the local area and nearby facilities for added safety.

Vehicle Inspection

Before starting your trip, perform a thorough inspection of the vehicle. Report any pre-existing damages or issues to the host for documentation.

Travel Insurance

Consider obtaining travel insurance for added protection. It can provide coverage for unexpected events and emergencies during your journey.

Public Reviews

Check public reviews and ratings of hosts or guests. Learn from others' experiences to make informed decisions when engaging in the community.
